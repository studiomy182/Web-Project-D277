About

Welcome to Explore Washington, your premier destination for uncovering the wonders of the Evergreen State. Founded with a passion for connecting people with the richness of Washington's landscapes, cultures, and experiences, our platform goes beyond traditional travel guides. Our mission is to inspire exploration, fostering a deeper understanding and appreciation for the diverse tapestry that makes Washington truly extraordinary.

At Explore Washington, we believe that every corner of this state has a story to tell. Whether you're intrigued by the thriving urban scenes, captivated by the serene natural beauty, or eager to delve into the unique histories of Washington's cities, we've crafted a guide that reflects the essence of each locale. Our team is committed to providing accurate, up-to-date information to empower you to make the most of your Washington experience.

From vibrant festivals and scenic trails to the heartwarming local tales, we aim to capture the spirit of Washington in every detail. Explore Washington is more than a guide; it's a community of enthusiasts, adventurers, and those who appreciate the beauty of discovery. Join us in celebrating the enchanting allure of the Evergreen State, and let us be your compass in navigating the richness that awaits across Washington's cities and landscapes.

Key State Facts

  • Capital: Olympia
  • Population: 7.739 million (2021)
  • Unemployment rate: 4.0% (Nov 2022)
  • Attorney general: Bob Ferguson (Democratic Party)
  • Governor: Jay Inslee (Democratic Party)
  • Major export: Fixed-wing aircraft
  • Senators: Patty Murray (Democratic Party), Maria Cantwell (Democratic Party)

Colleges and Universities

There are six public four-year colleges and universities, 18 private nonprofit schools, four private for-profit schools, and 30 junior and public colleges in Washington, according to NCES data.
